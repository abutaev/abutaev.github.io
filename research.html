Research Interests

My recent interest work is concerned with:

  • space of functions of bounded mean oscillation and its subspaces
  • analysis on metric measure spaces

If you are a second-year or higher UFV student interested to gain experience with math research, you are welcome to reach out to discuss possible research opportunities. Participating in a research project can strengthen your resume, especially if you are considering graduate studies. It can also help you to decide a future career path.
