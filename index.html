Bio

I am Almaz Butaev, an Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of the Fraser Valley. I received my PhD from Concordia University (Montréal) under the supervision of Professor Galia Dafni. Before coming to UFV, I was a postdoc at University of Calgary and a Visiting Professor at University of Cincinnati.  

Research 

My research interests lie in the areas of harmonic analysis, function spaces, analysis on metric measure spaces, Whitney problems and other areas of analysis .
